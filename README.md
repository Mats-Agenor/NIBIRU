This repository contains a numerical experiment of a simplified model of the
Solar System to which a fictitious planet (“Nibiru”) with a long orbital period,
small pericenter, and high mass is added. The script's objective is to integrate over tens of thousands
of years and track how orbital elements (semi-major axis, eccentricity, inclination, and
heliocentric distance) evolve over time, providing a qualitative visualization of
perturbations and dynamical instability.

When using this code, please cite the article Agenor et al. 2026 (DOI: xxx.xxx.xxx-xx).
